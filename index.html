Three-minute Training

【25】A strong typhoon hit Cebu and other parts of the central Philippines. Many towns were under water. At least 85 people died, and hundreds of thousands left their homes. In Cebu, at least 49 people died. Seventy-five people are missing, and 17 are hurt. People stayed on rooftops to be safe. Cars and big boxes moved quickly in the streets with the flood. The death number may go up. A military helicopter crashed on Mindanao island, south of Cebu. Six crew members died. The typhoon brought heavy rain and strong wind. Power was out in many places. Roads were blocked, and help was slow in some areas. People need clean water, food, and dry clothes. Many families need a safe place to sleep. Workers and volunteers are trying to give help and fix roads. It will take time to repair homes and schools. Many people are sad and afraid, but they hope for support.
